Weekly Roadmap — Alphabet in 10 Days
Days 1–2
Ա Բ Գ Դ Ե Զ Է Ը
First 8 letters. Focus on recognizing vowels: Ա (a), Ե (ye), Է (e), Ը (uh).
Days 3–4
Թ Ժ Ի Լ Խ Ծ Կ
Consonants including the uniquely Armenian sounds Խ (kh) and Ծ (ts).
Days 5–6
Հ Ձ Ղ Ճ Մ Յ Ն
Mid-alphabet. Learn Ձ (dz), Ղ (gh) — sounds unique to Armenian.
Days 7–8
Շ Ո Չ Պ Ջ Ռ Ս
Includes Շ (sh), Չ (ch), Ջ (j) — familiar sounds in new shapes.
Days 9–10
Վ Տ Ր Ց Ու Փ Ք Եվ Օ Ֆ
Final letters + ligatures. Review the full alphabet, write simple words.
Week 2+
Reading & Words
Start reading simple syllables, common words, then short sentences.

Tip: The Armenian alphabet is phonetic — once you know the letters, you can read anything. Invest your first 2 weeks entirely in alphabet mastery before worrying about grammar or vocabulary. Handwriting each letter (not just typing) dramatically improves retention.
