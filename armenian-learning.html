SESSION 2
Midday · 8 min
Recognition Drill — Match the Sound
⌄

Test your ability to recognize letters by their sounds. This reinforces the connection between sound and shape.

📝 Instructions:
  1. Go to the Quiz tab
  2. Make sure Letters mode is selected
  3. Try the "Sound → Char" direction — this is harder and builds real recognition
  4. You'll hear a sound and must pick the Armenian letter from 4 choices
  5. Aim for 80% accuracy or higher
💡 Tip: The "Sound → Char" direction is more challenging because you have to recall the shape from memory. This is exactly what you need for real reading.
SESSION 3
Evening · 10 min
Read Real Armenian Words
⌄

Now see your 4 letters in real Armenian words. Reading these words out loud cements your letter knowledge.

📝 Instructions:
  1. Go to the Alphabet tab
  2. Click on each of the 4 letters: Ա, Բ, Գ, Դ
  3. At the bottom of each letter detail, you'll see Example Words
  4. Try to read each word aloud using only the sounds you know
  5. Use the Phrases tab to hear native pronunciation
💡 Tip: Don't worry if you can't read the whole word yet. Just focus on recognizing your 4 new letters and saying their sounds.
SESSION 4
Night · 7 min
End-of-Day Review & Reflection
⌄

Spaced repetition is the most powerful learning technique. Reviewing today's letters before sleep helps lock them into memory.

📝 Instructions:
  1. Go to the Flashcards tab
  2. Select Letters deck
  3. Flip through each card and rate how well you know it: Hard / Got it / Easy
  4. Mark the 4 letters you learned today as "Got it" or "Easy"
  5. Take 2–3 minutes to just review them without pressure
💡 Reflection: Which letter felt easiest today? Which one needs more practice? Write your thoughts down — this helps your brain consolidate what you've learned.
Weekly Roadmap — Alphabet in 10 Days
Days 1–2
Ա Բ Գ Դ Ե Զ Է Ը
First 8 letters. Focus on recognizing vowels: Ա (a), Ե (ye), Է (e), Ը (uh).
Days 3–4
Թ Ժ Ի Լ Խ Ծ Կ
Consonants including the uniquely Armenian sounds Խ (kh) and Ծ (ts).
Days 5–6
Հ Ձ Ղ Ճ Մ Յ Ն
Mid-alphabet. Learn Ձ (dz), Ղ (gh) — sounds unique to Armenian.
Days 7–8
Շ Ո Չ Պ Ջ Ռ Ս
Includes Շ (sh), Չ (ch), Ջ (j) — familiar sounds in new shapes.
Days 9–10
Վ Տ Ր Ց Ու Փ Ք Եվ Օ Ֆ
Final letters + ligatures. Review the full alphabet, write simple words.
Week 2+
Reading & Words
Start reading simple syllables, common words, then short sentences.
